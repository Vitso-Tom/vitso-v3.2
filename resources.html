Technical HIPAA Training for Builders

Most PHI and PII training is written for clinical and administrative audiences. Technical teams face different risks. Data leaks through logs, traces, analytics, test fixtures, feature flags, migrations, and tickets.

We built this course to meet builders where they work. It explains when "safe" values become regulated in context, how to prevent exposure in everyday workflows, and what to do if something slips through.

Purpose-built for developers, SREs, data engineers, and product teams
Real technical scenarios with do-this-now guidance
Clear handling patterns and simple escalation checklists
Self-contained HTML you can run and adapt
29-question assessment to confirm understanding
Open source: Code (MIT), Content (CC BY 4.0)
