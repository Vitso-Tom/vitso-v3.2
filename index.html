Why this exists

Most PHI and PII training is written for clinical and administrative audiences. Technical teams face different risks. Data can leak through logs, traces, analytics, test fixtures, feature flags, migrations, and tickets.

We built this course to meet builders where they work. It explains when values become regulated in context, how to prevent exposure in everyday workflows, and what to do if something slips through. It is free and remixable and includes a 29 question check to confirm understanding.

Who it is for

  • Developers, SREs, data engineers, product
  • Real technical scenarios with do this now guidance
  • Clear handling patterns and simple escalation checklists
  • Self contained HTML you can run and adapt
