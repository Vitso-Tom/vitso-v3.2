Tom Smolinsky

Founder & Principal Consultant, Vitso

Tom Smolinsky is a technology and cybersecurity executive with more than 20 years of experience as a CISO, CIO, and SVP across both high-technology companies and highly regulated sectors such as healthcare and life sciences. He is the founder of Vitso, where he provides executive consulting and fractional leadership for organizations navigating growth, compliance, and digital transformation.

A CISSP since 2012, Tom is recognized for aligning technology investments with business outcomes and building resilient, secure, and scalable systems. His expertise spans cloud transformation, AI adoption strategies, and M&A integrations, alongside regulatory and compliance frameworks including HIPAA, SOC 2, NIST 800-171, 21 CFR Part 11, and GxP.

Tom's leadership approach focuses on building right-sized technology organizations that accelerate time-to-value while creating environments where top talent thrives. He is known for turning techies into technologists, fostering cultures that elevate technical teams into strategic business partners. Through Vitso, he brings this approach from his corporate leadership background to a broad range of clients, enabling them to scale with confidence while attracting and retaining the best people.

At his core, Tom is a principled technologist, balancing urgency with governance, enabling innovation without sacrificing security, and delivering strategies that are both visionary and pragmatic.

About this free training

Why this exists

While most PHI and PII training targets clinical and administrative audiences, technical teams face a distinct set of risks. In development workflows, sensitive data can surface unexpectedly through logs, traces, analytics, test fixtures, feature flags, migrations, and tickets.

We built this course to meet builders where they work. It explains when values become regulated in context, how to prevent exposure in everyday workflows, and what to do if something slips through. It is free and remixable and includes a 29-question check to confirm understanding.

Who it is for

  • Developers, SREs, data engineers, product
  • Real technical scenarios with do-this-now guidance
  • Clear handling patterns and simple escalation checklists
  • Self-contained HTML you can run and adapt
