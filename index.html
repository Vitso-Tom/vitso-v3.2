Why this exists

While most PHI and PII training targets clinical and administrative audiences, technical teams face a distinct set of risks. In development workflows, sensitive data can surface unexpectedly through logs, traces, analytics, test fixtures, feature flags, migrations, and tickets.

We built this course to meet builders where they work. It explains when values become regulated in context, how to prevent exposure in everyday workflows, and what to do if something slips through. It is free and remixable and includes a 29-question check to confirm understanding.

Security That Accelerates

Builders drive innovation (the Id), compliance sets boundaries (the Super Ego), and integrated technology and security operations strike the critical balance (the Ego) that makes both fully effective. When these forces work together, security becomes an enabler rather than a blocker.
